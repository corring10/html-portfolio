Best Movies According to Sean

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

A Few Good Men

Doesn't matter how many times I have watched it, if I see it on or available on demand I will watch it.

Count of Monte Cristo

One of my favorite Jim Cavisal movies. The plot twists, witty lines and the story of love that endures. A must have to watch.

Lord of the Rings: Two Towers

Great movies series overall. But I have to say the story just hits way differently here in Middle Earth!
